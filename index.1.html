Stay Safe
Safety Tips for Women
01

Save Emergency Numbers

Keep 1091, 100, and 112 saved on speed dial. Share your live location with a trusted person when going out.

02

Document Everything

Take screenshots, record conversations (where legal), and keep records of threats, messages, or incidents with dates.

03

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, leave immediately. Your safety is more important than being polite or avoiding conflict.

04

Know Your Rights

You have the right to file an FIR at any police station 24/7. Police cannot refuse to register your complaint.

05

Build a Support Network

Confide in at least one trusted friend, family member, or colleague. Isolation makes abuse worse — stay connected.

06

Plan an Exit Strategy

If in an abusive home, keep a bag ready with ID, money, and essentials. Know where to go if you need to leave fast.
